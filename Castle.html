Castle Plans

This is the build guide for the awesome mega-build Taneth on the Reddit PVE Server.

Materials

The castle should be mainly constructed from Smooth Stone and Stone Bricks - Use Cobble in random places to add a "patchy" feel to the walls and towers.

Outer wall turrets/Towers will have lava flowing inside the 3 wide portion that is exposed inside and outside the wall, to achieve this, stack stone brick stairs with stone brick half slabs in the middle.
